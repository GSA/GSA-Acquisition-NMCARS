Subpart 5205.4 - RELEASE OF INFORMATION

Parent topic: Part 5205 - PUBLICIZING CONTRACT ACTIONS

5205.404 Release of long-range acquisition forecasts.

Submit an annual long-range acquisition forecast, using the format provided in Annex 25, to DASN(P) by email at RDAJ&As.fct@navy.mil with the subject “[Activity Name] FAR 5.404 Long Range Acquisition Forecast” by June 20.

5205.404-1 Release procedures.

(a) Application. The agency head designates the HCA as the official who may, in addition to the agency head, release long-range acquisition estimates.
