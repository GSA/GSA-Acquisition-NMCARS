Subpart 5216.2 - FIXED-PRICE CONTRACTS

Parent topic: Part 5216 - TYPES OF CONTRACTS

5216.202-90 Firm-fixed-price contracts.

Consultation with DPC is required prior to the use of a firm-fixed-price (FFP) contract for a sole source acquisition with a value greater than $500 million for U.S. or combined U.S./FMS requirements for a major system as defined in FAR 2.101. This consultation should use the Peer Review process provided at NMCARS 5201.170.
