Subpart 5212.4 - CONTRACT ADMINISTRATION AND AUDIT SERVICES

Parent topic: Part 5212 - ACQUISITION OF COMMERCIAL ITEMS

5212.403 Termination

(c)(4) Termination for cause. Within five (5) calendar days after issuing the notice of the termination or a change in termination status, submit a copy of the FAPIIS entry required by FAR 42.1503(h)(1)(iii), including changes in status of terminations, to the AGC (AI) by email at aio@navy.mil with the subject “FAR 12.403(c)(4) – Termination for Cause” followed by the contract number”.
