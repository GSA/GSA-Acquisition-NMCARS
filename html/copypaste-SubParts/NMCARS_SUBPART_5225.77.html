SUBPART 5225.77 — ACQUISITIONS IN SUPPORT OF OPERATIONS IN AFGHANISTAN

Parent topic: PART 5225 FOREIGN ACQUISITION

5225.7799 Authority to acquire products and services (including construction) from Afghanistan or from countries along a major route of supply to Afghanistan.

When submitting the written determination to DPC, provide a courtesy copy to DASN(P) by email at RDAJ&As.fct@navy.mil with the subject “[Activity Name] DFARS 225.7799 (Class Deviation 2020-O0002) – CC: “Determination to acquire [products or services] from a [Central Asian state, Pakistan, or the South Caucasus].”
