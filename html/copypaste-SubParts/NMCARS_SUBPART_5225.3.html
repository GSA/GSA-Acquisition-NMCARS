SUBPART 5225.3 – Contracts Performed Outside the United States

Parent topic: PART 5225 FOREIGN ACQUISITION

5225.370 Contracts requiring performance or delivery in a foreign country.

(d) Contracting Officers shall address Theater Business Clearance (TBC) requirements when soliciting or awarding contracts or performing contract administration. Solicitations and contracts subject to TBC are those valued in excess of $150K and with performance greater than 30 days that require performance or delivery, including construction contracts, with the place of performance in Afghanistan.
