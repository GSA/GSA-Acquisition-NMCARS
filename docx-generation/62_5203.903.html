5203.903 Policy.

(5) When notified of a complaint of reprisal described in DFARS 203.903(1), the contracting

officer shall notify the Office of General Counsel by email at aio@navy.mil with a courtesy copy to DASN(P) by email at Policy@navy.mil with the subject “DFARS 203.903 – Notification of a Complaint of Reprisal.”
