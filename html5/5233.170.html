5233.170 Briefing requirement for protested acquisitions valued at $1 billion or more.

Within 5 days of receipt of the protest, submit the required protest notice to DASN(P) by email at usn.pentagon.asstsecnavrdadc.mbx.pabt@us.navy.mil with the subject “[Activity Name] PGI 233.170 - Protest [solicitation or contract number, as appropriate].”
