5239.001 Applicability.

(a) All requests for DOD CIO approval of acquisition of a data server farm, data center or information systems technology used in a data center shall be submitted to DASN(P) by email at usn.pentagon.asstsecnavrdadc.mbx.pabt@us.navy.mil with the subject “[Activity Name] FAR 39.001 - Data Farm/Center IT Approval.”
