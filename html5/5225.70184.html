5225.7018-4 Nonavailability determination.

(a)(3) When submitting the signed individual nonavailability determination or denial to USD(A&S), provide a courtesy copy to DASN(P) by email at RDAJ&As.fct@navy.mil with the subject “[Activity Name] DFARS 225.7018-4 – CC: Individual Nonavailability Determination.”

(b)(i) When submitting the request for class nonavailability determination to DPC, provide a courtesy copy to DASN(P) by email at RDAJ&As.fct@navy.mil with the subject “[Activity Name] DFARS 225.7018-4 – CC: Class Nonavailability Determination Request.”

Parent topic: 5225.7018 Restriction on acquisition of certain magnets and tungsten.
