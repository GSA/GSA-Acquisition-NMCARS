5249.402-8 Reporting Information.

Within five (5) calendar days after issuing the notice of the termination for default (T4D) or a change in termination status, submit a copy of the FAPIIS entry required by FAR 42.1503(h)(1)(iii), including changes in status of terminations, to the AGC(AI) by email at aio@navy.mil with the subject "FAR 49.402-8 - T4D" followed by the contract number.

Parent topic: 5249.402 Termination of fixed-price contracts for default.
