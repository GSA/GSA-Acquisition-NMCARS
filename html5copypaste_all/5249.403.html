5249.403 Termination of cost–reimbursement contracts for default.

(a) Within five (5) calendar days after issuing the notice of the termination or a change in termination status, submit a copy of the FAPIIS entry required by FAR 42.1503(h)(1)(iii), including changes in status of terminations, to the AGC(AI) by email at aio@navy.mil with the subject "FAR

49.402-8 - T4D" followed by the contract number.
